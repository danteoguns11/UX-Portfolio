About Me

I am a dynamic individual with a passion for problem-solving and creating exceptional user experiences. My journey into the world of design has been an exciting shift from my previous role as a Test Analyst.

My career began with a Bachelor's degree in Business Information Technology from the University of Kent, providing me with a strong analytical and problem-solving background. After that, I found myself immersed in the fast-paced environment of the banking industry, working as a Test Analyst at Lloyds Bank with the brilliant minds at Cognizant. While this role brought its own set of challenges and rewards, I couldn't help but feel that my true passion lay elsewhere.

The shift from testing to design is driven by that profound realisation that design is my true calling, a path that allows me to combine my technical acumen with a passion for enhancing user experiences. This led me to gain a UX Design certification by taking a design course at School of UX.
