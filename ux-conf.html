Dashboard Design

The UX Conf

Business Research

TheUXConf, a leading design conference, is dedicated to fostering creativity and collaboration within the UX design community. To enhance the experience of attendees, speakers, and organizers, we propose a comprehensive research initiative to develop a user-centric Booking Management Dashboard. This tool will streamline the conference booking process and provide valuable insights to optimise event planning and execution.


Problem Statement:

The current system for managing conference bookings lacks user-friendliness, resulting in inefficiencies and potential user frustration. As the conference continues to grow, we recognize the need to overhaul and optimize our booking management process.
